Software

Axil 🌻

Programmer, Spring 2020

Axil is a game about gardening... in space!

As a part of the CS490 Software Production Studio, I worked on a multidisciplinary team of 6 students tasked with creating an original video game. I learned how to use Godot game engine; taught my teammates various skills regarding programming; learned from my group mates in the ways of art, programming, and production; researched licensing; and collaborated on ideas to make Axil, an extraterrestrial plant growing simulation game.

Film

Queer Chocolatier 🌈

Director & Editor, 2020

A short documentary about Muncie's local LGBTQ+ sweets shop, The Queer Chocolatier! This is a production that I led during my time as president of the university film group, Fringed. It was an honor and an amazing time to get to work with the head queer herself, Morgan Roddy.

Home Sweet Home 🏡

Producer, Director, & Writer, 2020

A short film about a person suffering from agoraphobia who refuses to leave their house until strange things start happening and paranoia overcomes them. This is a production by the university film group, Fringed.

As president I worked with my fellow film club member, Aaron Dwyer, to create this concept, write this script, and direct this short horror flick!

Radio

International Power Hour 📻

Radio Host, February 2020

A short excerpt from the International Power Hour's show on February 8th, 2020.

Our show, The International Power Hour, is a two-hour long program exploring and celebrating the diversity in music from around the world! The International Power Hour is the longest running show on WCRD, Ball State's student run radio station. Having various hosts since 2015, I have been one of three hosts since the Spring of 2017. Diversifying the airwaves is our mission that we promise to always deliver!
